C2W2, METAVERSE RUNWAY EVENT, 2025
The C2W2 virtual runway on Hyperfy, accessible via VR or PC through the link hyperfy.io/c2w2, is a 30-minute metaverse event showcasing nearly 50 AI-designed avatars across six thematic parts, blending traditional art, virtual fashion, NFTs, and transhumanism. Through dramatic lighting and a minimalist setting, it explores identity, technology, and belonging in a fragmented, three-dimensional narrative.
"The C2W2 virtual runway, hosted on Hyperfy, is a 30-minute metaverse event divided into six distinct parts, showcasing nearly 50 avatar designs from the C2W2 collection. Set in a minimalist, dark environment beneath a circular structure, the runway is illuminated by dramatic lighting that shifts every 30 seconds, presenting a seamless narrative through a single, cohesive concept. Each part of the event explores a unique theme, weaving together art, technology, and identity in a thought-provoking display. The runway begins with Part 1: Evolution of Medium of Traditional Art, Paintings, where the concept of painting—one of the most traditional artistic mediums—is transformed into avatars. Inspired by masters like Francis Bacon and Matisse, these AI-assisted designs embody the avatarization of this classic medium. In Part 2: Just Design, Virtual Fashion, the focus shifts to pure digital design, exploring the boundaries of virtual fashion through diverse avatars that reflect varied curiosities and personalities. Part 3: Evolution of Medium of Art as NFT blends the essence of the first two parts, merging NFT-based artworks or iconic personalities with digital design. This section creates a hybrid space where traditional and virtual art converge. In Part 4: AI-Powered Design, the narrative takes a bold turn, as entirely AI-generated avatars disrupt the established aesthetic. Much like the internet and AI have historically reset creative paradigms, this part challenges the value of the preceding designs, rendering familiar palettes obsolete. Part 5: Transhumanism reflects on the dehumanizing potential of these new design possibilities, where both designer and user lose their identities. With subtle references to the earlier parts, this section elevates the concept in a minimalist, almost sacred manner, portraying a parametric, historical ascent—akin to humanity’s salvation or the rise of a holy avatar. Finally, Part 6: Evolution of Art as Mutant Meaning introduces no new avatars. Instead, it strategically reintroduces designs from the previous parts, reimagined in a way that feels distinct yet familiar. Unlike the cyclical revival of trends like 90s jeans or 2000s aesthetics, this return to the “essence” is a fragmented transformation—a 30-minute simulation of a century-long history. At its core, the C2W2 runway is not about avatars or clothing. It is a multi-dimensional narrative exploring the intersections of AI, the metaverse, blockchain, belonging, and identity. By simulating the formation of an idea or institution, the event invites reflection on how technology reshapes our understanding of self and society in a complex, three-dimensional storytelling experience. Sections: 1-Evolution of Medium of Traditional Art – Paintings 2-Just Design – Virtual Fashion 3-Evolution of Medium of Art as NFT 4-AI-Powered Design 5-Transhumanism 6-Evolution of Art as Mutant Meaning
