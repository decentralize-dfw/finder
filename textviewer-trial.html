Complete Purchase Guide: Wallet Setup, Minting, and Asset Management
Initial Wallet Setup and Configuration
1. Download and Install MetaMask
Visit metamask.io using Chrome, Firefox, or Brave browser
Click "Download" and select your browser extension
Follow the installation prompts
Create a new wallet:
Write down your 12-word recovery phrase on paper (never digitally)
Store securely in a physical location
Set a strong password
Confirm recovery phrase in correct order
2. Fund Your Wallet and Bridge to Base
Click the MetaMask extension icon
Copy your wallet address
Transfer ETH from an exchange or existing wallet to your MetaMask
Bridge your ETH to Base network:
Visit https://www.brid.gg/base?amount=&originChainId=1&token=ETH
Connect your MetaMask wallet
Enter the amount of ETH you want to bridge
Confirm the transaction
Wait for bridging completion (may take several minutes)
Verify your Base ETH balance in MetaMask
3. Marketplace Navigation and Purchase
Two ways to find DFW designs:
a) Through DFW Official Website:
Visit DFW's official website
Browse available designs
Click on desired design to view details
Connect your wallet when ready to purchase
b) Through OpenSea:
Search for DFW's collection
Browse available designs
Open the design's page
Connect your wallet when ready to purchase
4. Purchase Process
For Listed Items:
Click "Buy Now" (Note: Bidding is not available for listed items)
Confirm transaction in MetaMask
Wait for blockchain confirmation
For Unlisted Items:
Click "Make Offer" on the desired design
Set your offer amount
Submit the offer
Contact DFW through official social channels for further discussion
Await response and follow provided instructions
5. Asset Retrieval and Storage
Automatic blockchain confirmation sent to wallet
Receive private drive link containing:
Original GLB files
High-resolution renderings
Additional documentation
Download all materials immediately
Store backup copies securely
6. Metaverse Integration
Visit OnCyber or Hyperfy platforms
Connect same MetaMask wallet
Navigate to your collection
Verify GLB object appears
Test object spawning and placement
